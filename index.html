Jongse Park

Associate Professor

KAIST

I am an associate professor in the School of Computing (SoC) at KAIST and a faculty member of Computer Architecture and Systems Laboratory (CASYS). I am co-affiliated with School of Electrical Engineering, Graduate School of AI Semiconductor, Graduate School of System Architect, Graduate School of AI, and Department of Semiconductor System Engineering (SSE). I am very much looking forward to working with talented students on exciting projects. If you’re interested in joining my research group, please contact me via email introducing yourself (with your CV and transcripts if available).

I am currently on sabbatical, visiting the Pervasive Parallelism Lab at Stanford University.

Research Interests

  • Accelerating AI Serving Systems
  • Enabling On-Device AI Systems
  • Leveraging Processing-in-Memory for AI Systems
  • Designing Flexible AI Compiler Frameworks
